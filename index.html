Truong Minh Triet — Embedded Systems Engineer

$ cat about.txt

I design and program embedded systems where firmware meets hardware: microcontroller bring-up, RTOS task scheduling, low-power wireless, and the PCBs underneath. My focus spans robotics, IoT, and medical devices — with earlier automotive firmware work at Bosch — taking a board from schematic to shipping firmware.

I work close to the metal: SPI/I2C/CAN peripherals, sensor fusion, FOTA pipelines, and Zephyr/FreeRTOS drivers. I care about systems that are reliable, measurable, and documented — not just demos.
